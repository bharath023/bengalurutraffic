The mode shift paradox: On Aug 14, Namma Metro's best day, 8.40 lakh journeys were made across 83 stations. That same month, Karnataka added roughly 12,000–15,000 new vehicles per day to the road fleet. Every new peak day record Metro achieves is outpaced within weeks by vehicle registrations. The metro is growing — but the denominator (vehicles on road) grows faster.
